How Drink Counter Handles Data

Drink Counter is designed to keep your drink log on your device.

  • Your drink entries, calendar marks, and settings are stored locally on-device.
  • You can export your data to CSV only when you choose to do so.
  • Drink Counter does not require account creation.

What We Do Not Intentionally Collect

  • No name, email, or profile is required to use the app.
  • No location tracking.
  • No ad tracking or third-party advertising SDKs.
  • No cloud sync of your drink logs by default.

Apple Platform Data

Apple may process limited technical data related to App Store delivery, purchases, or diagnostics under Apple’s own policies. That processing is controlled by Apple and your device/account settings.

For details, see Apple’s privacy information in App Store Connect and Apple’s privacy policy.

Manage or Delete Data

  • Delete the app to remove local app data from your device.
  • Use in-app CSV export if you want a copy of your records before deletion.

Common Help

  • If the app appears out of sync, fully close and reopen it.
  • For history sharing, use Settings → Export CSV.
